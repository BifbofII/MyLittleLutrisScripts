Gamepad profiles for Tomb Raider 1 glATI.

TR1 glATI does not support directly gamepad, you have to install a third party software.
This software will attribute keyboard keys to your gamepad. 
Depend your gamepad, choose an app below, install it on your Linux distribution, import appropriate profile and launch TR1 glATI, you can now play the game with your gamepad.

x360 controller profile to use with antimicro Tomb Raider --> tr1_glati_x360.gamecontroller.amgp
You can download Antimicro here: https://github.com/AntiMicro/antimicro

Steam controller profile to use with sc-controller --> tr1_glati_steam.sccprofile
You can download sc-controller here --> https://github.com/kozec/sc-controller

You will find gamepad profiles in your Lutris TR1 glATI wineprefix: c:\TOMBATI\gamepad profiles\
